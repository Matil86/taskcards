# Privacy Policy for TaskCards

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## Introduction

TaskCards ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information (Optional - Only with Google Sign-In)

When you choose to sign in with Google, we collect:
- **Email address**
- **Name**
- **Profile picture**
- **Google User ID**

**Note:** Google Sign-In is **completely optional**. You can use TaskCards fully without signing in. We only collect this information if you explicitly choose to enable cloud sync.

### 2. Task Data

We store:
- **Task text content** (titles and descriptions you create)
- **Task metadata** (completion status, due dates, reminders, order)
- **List information** (if using multiple lists feature)

**Storage:**
- **Without Sign-In:** All task data is stored **locally on your device only**. We cannot access this data.
- **With Sign-In:** Task data is synced to Google Firebase Firestore under your user account. This enables cloud backup and multi-device sync.

### 3. Technical and Diagnostic Information

We automatically collect:
- **Crash reports** (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  - Device model and manufacturer
  - Android OS version
  - App version
  - Stack traces and error logs
  - Crash timestamp
- **Analytics data** (via Firebase Analytics)
  - App usage statistics
  - Feature usage patterns
  - Session duration
  - Device language and region settings
  - Screen resolution

### 4. Camera Permission

We request camera access **only when you use the QR code scanner feature** to import shared task lists. Camera data is:
- **Processed locally on your device**
- **Never uploaded to our servers**
- **Used only for QR code scanning**
- **Not stored or recorded**

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

1. **App Functionality**
   - Store and sync your tasks across devices (when signed in)
   - Send reminder notifications for due dates
   - Enable QR code scanning for task list sharing

2. **App Improvement**
   - Identify and fix bugs and crashes
   - Understand feature usage to prioritize improvements
   - Optimize app performance

3. **Legal Compliance**
   - Comply with applicable laws and regulations
   - Protect against fraud and abuse

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **Encryption in transit:** All data transmitted between your device and Firebase servers uses TLS/SSL encryption
- **Firebase Security Rules:** Cloud data is protected by Firebase authentication rules
- **No third-party access:** We do not sell or share your data with third parties

### Data Location

- **Local data:** Stored in app-specific storage on your Android device
- **Cloud data:** Stored in Google Firebase servers (see [Google Cloud Privacy](https://cloud.google.com/security/privacy))

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We **do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information** to third parties.

We share data only with:
- **Firebase (Google):** For cloud storage, authentication, analytics, and crash reporting
  - See [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
  - See [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

We may disclose information if required by law or to:
- Comply with legal processes
- Protect our rights and safety
- Prevent fraud or abuse

## Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

### 1. Access Your Data
- All task data is visible within the app
- Cloud data can be viewed in the Firebase console (contact us for assistance)

### 2. Delete Your Data

**Local Data:**
- Go to **Settings** → Clear app data, or uninstall the app

**Cloud Data:**
- Contact us at hippcomputing+taskcards@gmail.com to request account deletion
- We will delete all your cloud data within 30 days

### 3. Export Your Data
- Use the app's "Share" feature to share a list access link (deep link URL)
- Contact us for a complete data export of your task data

### 4. Opt-Out of Analytics
- Currently, analytics are essential for app improvement and cannot be disabled
- You can opt out by using the app without signing in

### 5. Revoke Google Sign-In
- Go to **Settings** → Sign out
- Your local data remains on your device
- Cloud sync will stop immediately

## Children's Privacy

TaskCards is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

## International Users (GDPR Compliance)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR:

- **Right to access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Right to rectification:** Correct inaccurate personal data
- **Right to erasure:** Request deletion of your personal data
- **Right to restrict processing:** Limit how we use your data
- **Right to data portability:** Receive your data in a structured format
- **Right to object:** Object to processing of your personal data
- **Right to withdraw consent:** Withdraw consent at any time

**Legal Basis for Processing:**
- **Consent:** You explicitly opt-in to Google Sign-In
- **Legitimate Interest:** Analytics and crash reporting for app improvement

To exercise these rights, contact us at hippcomputing+taskcards@gmail.com.

## Data Retention

- **Task Data:** Retained indefinitely until you delete it or uninstall the app
- **Cloud Data:** Retained until you request deletion or delete your account
- **Analytics Data:** Retained for 14 months (Firebase default)
- **Crash Reports:** Retained for 90 days (Firebase default)
